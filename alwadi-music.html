About Us

AlWadi Musical Instruments

AlWadi Musical Instruments was founded and is run by Bishara Deeb, a professional singer and oud player who has performed on stages for decades, as well as a calligraphy artist. When his deep musical career met his skilled carpentry roots, he became one of the rare master oud builders in the region.

Our store is renowned not only for selling premium instruments but also for expert instrument repairs. It is a living workshop where authentic ouds are meticulously built by hand, and custom arabic calligraphy is personally drawn and sold.
